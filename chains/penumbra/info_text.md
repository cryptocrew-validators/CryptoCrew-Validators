## About Penumbra

Penumbra is a fully private, Proof-of-Stake Layer-1 blockchain for the Cosmos ecosystem, where all transactions — including swaps, staking, and transfers — are shielded by default using zero-knowledge proofs. It features a private DEX with sealed-bid batch auctions to prevent MEV, and integrates with IBC to bring privacy to cross-chain asset flows. UM is the native token used for staking, governance, and transaction fees.

## How to earn rewards by staking UM

Stake UM tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards proportional to your stake. Staking and rewards are fully shielded, preserving privacy throughout. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to an unbonding period.
