## About Namada

Namada is a Proof-of-Stake Layer-1 blockchain focused on composable, multichain privacy. It provides a shielded asset hub using zk-SNARKs, enabling users to shield any asset — including tokens and NFTs from other chains — and perform private cross-chain transactions via IBC. Built in Rust with CometBFT consensus, Namada acts as a privacy layer that can be retrofitted onto existing chains and protocols without requiring changes to those ecosystems. NAM is the native token used for staking, governance, and shielded rewards.

## How to earn rewards by staking NAM

Stake NAM tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards proportional to your stake. Shielded staking rewards provide additional privacy incentives. Rewards can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to an unbonding period.
