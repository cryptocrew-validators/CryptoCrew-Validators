## About Agoric

Agoric is a Proof-of-Stake blockchain in the Cosmos ecosystem. Agoric’s JavaScript smart contract platform offers 17M+ developers a safe, reusable library of DeFi components to rapidly build and deploy dapps, NFTs, and markets. BLD is the native Agoric staking token, while IST is an over-collateralized stable token used for fees on the Agoric platform.

## How to earn rewards by staking BLD

Stake BLD tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
