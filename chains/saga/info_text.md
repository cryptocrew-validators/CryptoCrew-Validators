## About Saga

Saga is a Proof-of-Stake Layer-1 protocol built on the Cosmos SDK that makes launching dedicated application chains as easy as deploying a smart contract. Its Pegasus architecture separates concerns into three layers: a security chain (where SAGA is staked and slashed), a platform chain (which aggregates and forwards security via Cross-Chain Validation), and Chainlets (the actual app chains where computation happens). Through its Realms framework, Saga supports VM-agnostic deployments — including EVM-based Ethlets and integrations with Polygon, Avalanche, and Celestia — giving developers a fully automated, gasless, and interoperable chain-launching experience. SAGA is the native token used for staking, securing Chainlets, and governance.

## How to earn rewards by staking SAGA

Stake SAGA tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network inflation rewards proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
