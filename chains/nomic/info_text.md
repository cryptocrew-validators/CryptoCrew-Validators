## About Nomic

Nomic is a Proof-of-Stake blockchain that brings native Bitcoin to the Cosmos ecosystem through nBTC — a trustless, IBC-compatible Bitcoin token backed 1:1 by real BTC held in a decentralized reserve. Users can deposit Bitcoin into the Nomic bridge and receive nBTC, which can then be used freely across Cosmos DeFi without relying on a centralized custodian. NOM is the native token used for staking, governance, and securing the Bitcoin bridge.

## How to earn rewards by staking NOM

Stake NOM tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards including a portion of Bitcoin bridge fees paid in nBTC. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
