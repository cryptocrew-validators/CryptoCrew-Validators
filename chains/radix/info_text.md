## About Radix

Radix is a Layer-1 blockchain purpose-built for DeFi, featuring a unique asset-oriented programming model through its Scrypto language and the Radix Engine. Unlike account-based or EVM chains, Radix treats tokens as native first-class assets with built-in guarantees — making a whole class of smart contract exploits impossible by design. Its Cerberus consensus protocol is designed for linear scalability through sharding without sacrificing atomic composability across shards. XRD is the native token used for staking, governance, and network fees.

## How to earn rewards by staking XRD

Stake XRD tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network emission rewards proportional to your stake. Rewards are distributed each epoch and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to an unbonding period of approximately 1–2 weeks.
