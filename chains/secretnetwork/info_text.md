## About Secret Network

Secret Network is a Proof-of-Stake blockchain built on the Cosmos SDK that provides programmable privacy for Web3. It features Secret Contracts — smart contracts that can process encrypted inputs, outputs, and state — enabling confidential DeFi, private NFTs, and data ownership applications. Secret Network connects to other Cosmos chains via IBC and to Ethereum via its Axelar-powered bridge, bringing privacy to the broader ecosystem. SCRT is the native token used for staking, governance, gas fees, and powering Secret Contract computations.

## How to earn rewards by staking SCRT

Stake SCRT tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
