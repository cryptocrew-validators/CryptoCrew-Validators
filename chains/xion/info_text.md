## About XION

XION is a Proof-of-Stake Layer-1 blockchain built on the Cosmos SDK, purpose-built for mainstream adoption through its Generalized Abstraction layer. By integrating account, signature, fee, and interoperability abstractions directly at the protocol level, XION removes the need for seed phrases, gas tokens, and blockchain-specific UX — letting users sign in with email or biometrics and pay fees in stablecoins like USDC. This makes it uniquely suited for consumer apps, gaming, and e-commerce that require Web2-level onboarding. XION is the native token used for staking, governance, fee offsetting, and collateral within the ecosystem.

## How to earn rewards by staking XION

Stake XION tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards proportional to your stake. Staking inflation is calculated only against staked tokens (not total supply), resulting in more sustainable economics. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
