## About Persistence

Persistence One is a Proof-of-Stake blockchain built on the Cosmos SDK, focused on BTCFi liquidity infrastructure. It operates a DEX optimized for near-zero-slippage swaps between BTC variants and BTCFi tokens, addressing the liquidity fragmentation that comes with the growing number of wrapped and liquid Bitcoin assets across chains. XPRT is the native token used for staking, governance, and fee distribution on the Persistence DEX.

## How to earn rewards by staking XPRT

Stake XPRT tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and DEX fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
