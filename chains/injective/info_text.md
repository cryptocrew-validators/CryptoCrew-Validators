## About Injective

Injective is an interoperable Layer-1 blockchain built for DeFi, offering on-chain financial infrastructure modules for decentralized exchanges, prediction markets, and lending protocols. It features native cross-chain bridging to both EVM chains (Ethereum) and non-EVM chains (Solana, Cosmos), a fully on-chain order book, and MEV-resistant transaction ordering. INJ is the native token used for staking, governance, protocol fee burning, and collateral across DeFi applications.

## How to earn rewards by staking INJ

Stake INJ tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
