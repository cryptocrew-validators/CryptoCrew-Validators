## About Celestia

Celestia is a modular data availability (DA) network that decouples data availability from execution and consensus, allowing developers to launch new blockchains with minimal overhead. By using data availability sampling (DAS), light nodes can verify block availability without downloading the full block, enabling the network to scale with the number of users. TIA is the native token used for paying for blobspace, staking, and governance.

## How to earn rewards by staking TIA

Stake TIA tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
