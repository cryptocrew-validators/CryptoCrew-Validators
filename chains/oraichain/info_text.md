## About Oraichain

Oraichain is a Proof-of-Stake Layer-1 blockchain and the world's first AI-powered oracle network, connecting Artificial Intelligence APIs to smart contracts across multiple chains. Unlike traditional data oracles that relay raw data, Oraichain uses AI models executed in virtual machines to generate and validate data on-chain, enabling a new class of AI-driven DeFi and Web3 applications. Its ecosystem includes an AI API marketplace, data hub, and DeFi protocols powered by on-chain AI inference. ORAI is the native token used for staking, governance, and paying for AI oracle services.

## How to earn rewards by staking ORAI

Stake ORAI tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
