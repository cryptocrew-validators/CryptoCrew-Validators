## About ZigChain

ZigChain is a Proof-of-Stake Layer-1 blockchain built on the Cosmos SDK, focused on decentralized wealth management and real-world asset (RWA) tokenization. Originating from the Zignaly ecosystem, it provides a modular "Wealth Management Engine" that enables developers to build investment protocols, copy-trading products, and asset management applications directly on-chain. ZIG is the native token used for staking, governance, and powering wealth management protocols.

## How to earn rewards by staking ZIG

Stake ZIG tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
