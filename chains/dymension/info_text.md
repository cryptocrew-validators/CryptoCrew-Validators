## About Dymension

Dymension is a Proof-of-Stake blockchain that serves as a settlement and coordination layer for RollApps — easy-to-deploy application-specific rollups that inherit security from Dymension's hub. It enables developers to launch their own high-performance, customizable chains in minutes while staying connected to Dymension's shared liquidity and IBC ecosystem. DYM is the native token used for staking, governance, and network fees.

## How to earn rewards by staking DYM

Stake DYM tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
