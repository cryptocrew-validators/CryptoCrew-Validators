## About Union

Union is a zero-knowledge Proof-of-Stake blockchain and interoperability layer that connects Cosmos, Ethereum, and EVM ecosystems without relying on trusted intermediaries or multisigs. It uses ZK light client proofs to verify consensus across chains, enabling trustless cross-chain asset transfers and message passing with cryptographic security guarantees. Union's architecture is designed to be the foundational trust-minimized bridge layer for the entire modular blockchain ecosystem. UNO is the native token used for staking, governance, and securing the network.

## How to earn rewards by staking UNO

Stake UNO tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
