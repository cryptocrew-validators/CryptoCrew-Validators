## About Terra

Terra is a Proof-of-Stake blockchain built on the Cosmos SDK, relaunched in 2022 following the collapse of the original Terra/LUNA ecosystem. The new chain focuses on rebuilding a developer-friendly smart contract platform using CosmWasm, without an algorithmic stablecoin. It maintains IBC connectivity to the broader Cosmos ecosystem and provides a foundation for dApps migrating from or building on the Terra ecosystem. LUNA is the native token used for staking, governance, and paying transaction fees.

## How to earn rewards by staking LUNA

Stake LUNA tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards (block rewards and fees) proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to a 21-day unbonding period.
