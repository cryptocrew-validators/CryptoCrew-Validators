## About Monad

Monad is a high-performance Layer-1 blockchain that is fully EVM-compatible while achieving 10,000+ TPS through a combination of parallel execution, a custom consensus protocol (MonadBFT), and a purpose-built database (MonadDB). Unlike standard EVM chains that process transactions sequentially, Monad executes them in parallel while maintaining full Ethereum compatibility — meaning existing Solidity contracts and tooling work without modification. MON is the native token used for staking, governance, and network fees.

## How to earn rewards by staking MON

Stake MON tokens by delegating them to validators using a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards proportional to your stake. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to an unbonding period.
