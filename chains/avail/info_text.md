## About Avail

Avail is a modular blockchain infrastructure project combining two core layers: Avail DA, a high-throughput data availability layer using KZG polynomial commitments and data availability sampling (DAS) to let rollups and modular chains scale without sacrificing security; and Avail Nexus, a cross-chain intent engine that unifies liquidity, users, and applications across ecosystems through a single integration — no bridges or redeployments required. AVAIL is the native token used for staking, governance, and network fees.

## How to earn rewards by staking AVAIL

Stake AVAIL tokens by nominating validators through a supported wallet. In return, you earn a share of network rewards proportional to your stake. Rewards can be claimed anytime, while unstaking is subject to an unbonding period.
